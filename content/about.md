## About This Blog

This blog documents what breaks when you run AI agents in production.

I'm Jason Wu. I build AI systems — agent fleets, pipelines, infrastructure — and write about the real problems I encounter: failed deployments, broken pipelines, verification gaps, and the gap between what AI agents promise and what they actually deliver.

No brochures. No vendor blog posts. Just the unfiltered engineering reality of building with AI.

## About the Author

Jason Wu is a technologist building autonomous AI agent systems. He runs ZYou Labs, where he develops the Hermes Agent platform and its surrounding ecosystem of tools, pipelines, and infrastructure.
